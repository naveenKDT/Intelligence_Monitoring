# Company & Industry Intelligence Platform

## Project Goal

Build an AI-powered Company and Industry Intelligence Platform that continuously collects, organizes, analyzes, and monitors information about companies, industries, products, services, technologies, engineering domains, markets, customers, competitors, and business activities.

The platform should create a structured knowledge base of companies and provide deep intelligence about what companies actually do, what industries they operate in, what capabilities they have, how they are growing, and how they relate to other companies.

The platform should allow users to discover companies, understand industries, analyze markets, identify competitors, monitor company activities, and search company knowledge using natural language.

---

# Core Concept

This is not a website scraper.

This is a Company Knowledge Platform.

The system continuously collects company information from public sources and transforms unstructured content into structured business intelligence.

Every company becomes a living profile that grows over time and maintains historical intelligence.
